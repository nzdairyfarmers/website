About NZ Dairy Farmers

NZ Dairy Farmers began as a Facebook group 15 years ago with a simple goal: connect farmers across New Zealand so they could share practical advice, ask questions and support each other. Over time the group has grown into one of the largest farming communities online in the country.

Our moderators and community

The community is supported by volunteer moderators who have invested a great deal of their own time to keep the space helpful, respectful and relevant. That volunteer effort is what has kept the community authentic and farmer-focused.

Partnerships and collaboration

We are now looking for responsible partners — people, companies and organisations — who want to engage directly and respectfully with our community. Potential partnership activities include:

  • Sponsorships of posts or events that bring clear value to farmers
  • Educational content and practical guides written for farmers
  • Field days, workshops or product demos coordinated with moderators
  • Targeted community trials or surveys (conducted transparently and with consent)

We prioritise partnerships that benefit farmers, respect the community's independence, and are transparent about any commercial interests.

To discuss partnership opportunities, email us at hi@dairy.org.nz.
