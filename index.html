About our community

Our Facebook group (facebook.com/groups/nzdairy) is one of the biggest farming communities in New Zealand. It was started 15 years ago to connect farmers around the country and has grown organically through the time and effort of our volunteer moderators.

Moderators have spent a lot of their own time building an authentic and useful space for farmers to ask questions, share experience, and support one another. As the group has grown, we are seeking partnerships to help engage and grow the community further while preserving its independent, farmer-first culture.

Who we connect with

Farmers, rural professionals, service providers and organisations that work with New Zealand dairy communities.

Partnership aim

We partner with companies and organisations looking to responsibly engage and market to our community — from sponsorships and events to content partnerships and product trials.

Why partner with us

Access an engaged, real-world farming audience who trust peer recommendations and practical advice. We prioritise authentic, useful partnerships that bring value to farmers.
