NZ DAIRY ASSOCIATION

Connecting New Zealand dairy farmers.

The NZ Dairy Association group is one of the biggest farming communities in New Zealand. It is a place where farmers, rural professionals, and industry voices can connect, share advice and stay close to the issues that matters.

Join the conversation Partner With Us
Community

A hub for farmers.

We started as a Facebook group in 2011 with a simple goal: to connect dairy farmers across New Zealand. Since then we have grown into one of the largest farming communities in the country, a place where members can share advice, start conversations, and support one another..

💬

Discussions

Members use the group to ask questions, share experiences and talk about the issues that matter to them.

🤝

Connections

Rural life can be isolating. We connect people with those who understand farming.

📣

Visibility

For organisations and rural businesses, it offers a direct way to be visible in their relevant community.

Partnerships

Looking to engage?

We’re open to working with people, companies and organisations who share our values. If you have a product, service or message that can genuinely help our community, we’d love to hear from you.

How we can work together

We’re interested in partnerships that create value for both our community and our partners — not just visibility, but meaningful engagement.

📢
Community initiatives

Collaborate on projects, events or promoting awareness on issues that affect dairy farmers.

🧠
Thought leadership

Share useful advice, research or educational content that helps the community.

🌱
Sponsored visibility

Promote relevant products, services or campaigns to a highly targeted and engaged audience.

Who this is for

We’re keen to hear from businesses and organisations whose work overlaps with dairy farming, rural communities or agricultural decision-makers.

  • Agri-businesses, rural suppliers and service providers
  • Farm technology, machinery and equipment companies
  • Animal health, feed, genetics and farm management providers
  • Financial, insurance, training and advisory organisations
  • Industry bodies, research groups and agricultural organisations
  • Brands or campaigns looking to reach the farming community in a relevant way

Get in touch.

Whether you’re a farmer wanting to connect with the group, or a business or organisation looking to partner with us, we’d love to hear from you.
